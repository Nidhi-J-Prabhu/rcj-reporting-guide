2025–26 Project Reporting Guide
NOTE: All projects must be reported within 3 days of the event’s end date. Timely documentation helps us preserve our journey and legacy!

This reporting process involves two simple things:

  1. Uploading all event pictures
  2. Sharing the key project details through the form

Step 1 – Upload photos to Kwikpic

  • Go to kwikpic.in and sign in.
  • Click on "Create a group" and name it after your project in the "Enter group name" area.
  • Set privacy to "Small personal group" and click "Create a group".
  • You’ll see your group on the homepage – click it, then click "Start uploading" to upload pictures.
  • Open the project folder and click the link icon (top right) to generate an invite link.
  • Copy the full access link and share it with nidhijprabhu03@gmail.com in the reporting form.

Step 2 – Make a report in Google Docs

  • Refer to: RCJ Reporting Format
  • Click on File – Make a copy and rename it with your project title.
  • Edit the document with all required details.
  • Copy the shareable link (with viewer access).

Step 3 – Fill the reporting form

  • Once you have your Kwikpic link and Google Doc ready – go to the form below.
  • Fill in the required fields and paste both links where needed.

Go to Project Reporting Form
